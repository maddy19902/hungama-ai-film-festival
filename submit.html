SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

ELIGIBILITY

Submissions must utilize AI-generated content for at least 50% of the visual pipeline. All generative tools are permitted. Work must have been completed after January 1, 2024.

TECHNICAL SPECS

  • Resolution: 4K UHD (3840x2160)
  • Format: ProRes 422 HQ / H.264
  • Audio: 5.1 Surround or Stereo
  • Max Size: 10 GB

SUBMISSION FEE

Standard entry fee is $25 USD per film. Late submissions incur an additional $15 fee. Student waivers available upon request with valid ID.

RIGHTS & USAGE
