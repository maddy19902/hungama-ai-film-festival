Submission Guidelines

Eligibility

Films must be created using AI technology or be AI-native productions. Works created by AI without human input are also eligible. Submit works created after January 1, 2024.

Technical Requirements

Video format: MP4, MOV, or WebM. Resolution: Minimum 1080p (1920 x 1080). Frame rate: 24fps, 25fps, or 30fps. Audio: Clear, properly mixed, and in English or with subtitles.

Submission Fee

Submission fee is $50 USD for the first film. Each additional film: $30 USD. Fee waiver requests available for emerging filmmakers from underrepresented regions.

Deadline

All submissions must be received by May 31, 2026 at 11:59 PM UTC. Late submissions will not be accepted. Notification of selection by July 15, 2026.

Rights & Distribution

By submitting, you grant Hungama AI Film Festival the right to screen, archive, and promote your film. All intellectual property remains with the creator. Commercial distribution rights remain with the filmmaker.

Questions?

Contact us at submissions@hungamafestival.com for any inquiries about the submission process.
